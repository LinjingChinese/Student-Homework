学生 (Student) 请问，超市怎么走？ qǐngwèn, chāoshì zěnme zǒu? Excuse me, how do I get to the supermarket?
🚶
路人 (Passerby) 超市有点远，你可以坐出租车去。 chāoshì yǒudiǎn yuǎn, nǐ kěyǐ zuò chūzūchē qù. The supermarket is a bit far, you can take a taxi.

学生 (Student) 我的手机号是：138-1111-1919。 wǒ de shǒujīhào shì: yāo sān bā - yāo yāo yāo yāo - yāo jiǔ yāo jiǔ. My phone number is 138-1111-1919.
🛒 到了超市门口 (Arrived at the supermarket...)

📖 阅读练习：《大卫去超市》 (Reading)

大卫是一个大学生，他刚刚来到这所学校学习。 Dàwèi shì yíge dàxuésheng, tā gānggāng láidào zhè suǒ xuéxiào xuéxí. David is a college student, he just arrived at this school to study.
一天，他坐出租车去超市买东西。 Yìtiān, tā zuò chūzūchē qù chāoshì mǎi dōngxi. One day, he took a taxi to the supermarket to buy things.
他买了很多零食、面包和牛奶。 Tā mǎile hěnduō língshí, miànbāo hé niúnǎi. He bought many snacks, bread, and milk.
在买单的时候，大卫问：“可以扫码支付吗？” Zài mǎidān de shíhou, Dàwèi wèn: "Kěyǐ sǎomǎ zhīfù ma?" When paying, David asked: "Can I pay by scanning a QR code?"
营业员说：“可以。但是你要先注册。” Yíngyèyuán shuō: "Kěyǐ. Dànshì nǐ yào xiān zhùcè." The clerk said: "Yes, but you need to register first."
大卫用手机号注册了会员，他收到一个验证码：115 281。 Dàwèi yòng shǒujīhào zhùcè le huìyuán, tā shōudàole yíge yànzhèngmǎ: yāo yāo wǔ, èr bā yāo. David registered as a member and received a code: 115 281.
付完钱，他坐出租车回学校了。 Fùwán qián, tā zuò chūzūchē huí xuéxiào le. After paying, he took a taxi back to school.
